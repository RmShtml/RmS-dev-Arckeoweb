Description :

An incomplete copper alloy Crotal bell of a single cast form of Post-Medieval date (AD 1550-1750). The object comprises two hemispheres divided by a raised horizontal circumferential rib. There are two sound holes one on either side of the integral rectangular suspension loop which would have projected from the upper hemisphere, and is now composed of two short spurs due to old, abraded breaks. There is a minor missing portion within the upper hemisphere around one of the sound holes. The lower sound hemisphere is composed of two circular sound holes close to the ridge between which is a rectangular sound slot. The lower hemisphere is decorated with a radiating 'sunburst' pattern to either side of the sound slot. The iron pea is retained within.
