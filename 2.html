Description :

A complete early Medieval, Anglo-Saxon, rock quartz bead, Group F1 (Haughton and Powlesland 1999), c. AD 501-600. It is globular and bicone in shape, with one flat face. It is colourless with perforations from each end which meet in the middle. There are some cracks, chips, and wear. The object is part of a group of finds from the same area which suggest a 6th century furnished inhumation burial and with two other beads
